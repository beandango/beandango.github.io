Here at ███████ ███████████, we are no stranger to peculiar subjects and intriguing findings. This page centers around our ongoing research and experiences with the subject DG-001, codenamed "Dango".

Most notably are her two golden eyes. One on her chest, and one on her right thigh.

We still have no idea what these are for, if for normal vision purposes, or something more complicated than that.
