The Craftsman

At the home of ██████ ████, who we will shorthand to "The craftsman", there are a number of figures scattered all about. Some clearly polished, painted, painstakingly rendered to extraordinary detail.

Some, are clearly still works in progress. Half-sculpted, dissassembled body-parts strewn across his workbench.

The closer we move to his computer desk, however, the less professionally crafted his figures become.

We found several smaller clay figures, no, dolls of what looks to be Dango, as well as a plushie which researchers here are speculating might be an earlier version of this character.

The discovery of these Dango-esque dolls leaves us with the conclusion that he may truly have had something to do with the Dango's sudden appearance after all.
